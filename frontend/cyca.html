Central YCA (CYCA)

The Central Young Chakma Association (CYCA) serves as the apex governing body, providing leadership, direction, and oversight to the entire organization.

Governance and Oversight

The CYCA is responsible for supervising all Zonal Committees, which in turn oversee the Branch Committees within their respective regions. Additionally, a select number of Branch Committees are directly affiliated with and managed by the Central YCA to ensure streamlined communication and direct implementation of key initiatives.
