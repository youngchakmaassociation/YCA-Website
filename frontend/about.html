About the Young Chakma Association

Uniting and empowering Chakma youth for a brighter future.

Our Mission & Vision

To unite and empower the Chakma youth by fostering a strong sense of community, preserving our cultural heritage, and promoting educational and leadership opportunities. We envision a future where every young Chakma individual is an active, informed, and proud contributor to our society and the world.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate university students, the Young Chakma Association (YCA) was born from a shared desire to create a unified voice for the Chakma youth. In our early days, we focused on organizing small cultural gatherings and educational workshops, laying the groundwork for a community that was both connected and informed.

Over the years, YCA has grown from a small collective into a robust organization with members across the country. Key milestones include launching our annual youth leadership summit in 2015, establishing a scholarship fund for underprivileged students in 2018, and partnering with international cultural bodies to showcase Chakma heritage on a global stage. Each step has been driven by our commitment to empowering the next generation of Chakma leaders.
