Membership Tiers & Eligibility

school

Student Member

For current students

Open to any individual of Chakma descent currently enrolled in a high school, college, or university program. Perfect for young individuals looking to connect and grow.

  • check_circle Access to all YCA workshops and cultural events.
  • check_circle Eligibility for student mentorship programs.
  • check_circle Discounted rates on special ticketed events.
$10 / year
work

Professional Member

For working professionals

Popular

For individuals of Chakma descent who are in the workforce. This tier is designed to foster professional connections and provide leadership opportunities.

  • check_circle All benefits of Student Membership.
  • check_circle Exclusive access to professional networking events.
  • check_circle Opportunity to become a mentor for student members.
  • check_circle Voting rights in YCA general elections.
$30 / year
